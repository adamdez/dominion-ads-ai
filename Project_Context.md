# Project Context

## Business
Dominion Home Deals is a real estate wholesaling / acquisitions business.

This is NOT a traditional real estate agent or brokerage project.
This system must be designed for wholesaling economics and wholesaling operations.

## Markets
Primary markets:
- Spokane County, Washington
- Kootenai County / Coeur d'Alene / North Idaho

These are separate markets and should be treated separately in:
- campaign structure
- landing pages
- reporting
- AI recommendations
- call tracking
- performance analysis

## Business Goal
Generate first-party motivated seller leads and reduce dependence on pay-per-lead providers.

The goal is to capture seller leads directly, respond faster, qualify better, and convert more deals internally.

## What Success Means
This system should optimize for:
- qualified seller leads
- appointments
- offers
- contracts
- closed deals
- revenue per source

This system should NOT optimize only for:
- clicks
- CTR
- cheap form fills
- raw lead volume

North-star business metrics:
- cost per qualified lead
- cost per appointment
- cost per offer
- cost per contract
- revenue per channel
- revenue per deal source

## Seller Situations
The business targets distressed or motivated seller situations, including:
- inherited property
- probate / estate
- tired landlord
- tenant issues
- major repairs
- foundation / mold / damage
- divorce
- foreclosure / pre-foreclosure
- relocation
- vacant property
- low-intent / just exploring

AI and reporting should classify leads and search terms by seller situation whenever possible.

## Marketing Strategy
Primary acquisition channel first:
- Google Search

Later channels:
- Google retargeting
- Meta retargeting
- Meta prospecting only after Search economics are proven

Search comes first because it captures active seller intent.

## Domain Strategy
Primary brand and authority site:
- dominionhomedeals.com

Other owned domains are support assets, not separate full businesses.
They may be used for:
- redirects
- paid search landing tests
- short offline URLs
- future experiments

Do not assume multiple full sites should be launched.

## Product Goal
Build a Google Ads operator system inside the CRM.

The system should:
- sync Google Ads data
- store lead and deal outcomes
- generate AI recommendations
- show a human-friendly operator dashboard
- support approval workflows
- log all changes and actions
- eventually support controlled automation

## AI Role
AI is an operator assistant, not an unrestricted account manager.

AI should:
- analyze performance
- identify waste
- identify opportunities
- classify search terms
- classify seller situations
- generate recommendations
- assign risk levels
- estimate likely impact

AI should NOT automatically make risky changes without approval.

## Speed to Lead
This business competes against PPL companies partly through speed.

The system should support:
- instant acknowledgment
- fast SMS follow-up
- quick call attempt
- AI-assisted qualification
- call transcript review
- lead scoring

## Build Priorities
Build in this order:
1. data model / schema
2. Google Ads data sync
3. search term mining engine
4. lead and deal attribution
5. recommendation queue
6. operator page UI
7. approval and audit logging
8. offline conversion feedback
9. landing page testing
10. later channel expansion

## Build Principles
- simple first
- production-oriented
- typed code
- audit everything
- no fabricated APIs
- no overengineering
- data before automation
